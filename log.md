## Log
I began by pasting the api and building my foundation, which was to have a pokemon teambuilder and opponent selector so that you can see how your team does and why it does good/bad.

I decided to add more detailed information on each pokemon and opponent, with move selections, abilities, levels, items, etc. I also wanted the pixel png of each pokemon to be visible as you searched, and added a suggestion area beneath the searchbar. 

I created a logo and formatted the theme, and refined the matchup analysis to factor thing other than just typing.

I used the pokeapi to fetch data on each pokemon and each move, as well as the small pixel logos.
